Our Story

Fifteen years of quiet, careful work.

Founded in Jaipur in 2009, Trinetra Detective Agency grew from a small team of former intelligence and law-enforcement professionals into one of Rajasthan's most trusted investigation firms.

Our name — Trinetra, the third eye — reflects our promise: to perceive the truth that lies hidden beneath the surface. Over 5,000 cases later, that promise still guides every investigation we take on, whether a sensitive matrimonial enquiry or a complex corporate fraud.
