Questions

Surveillance FAQ

Is private surveillance legal in Jaipur?
Observing a person in public spaces and documenting lawful evidence is legal. We never trespass, tap phones or hack devices — all our methods comply with Indian law.
Will the subject know they are being watched?
No. Our agents are trained in covert observation and rotate to avoid detection. The entire operation is designed to be invisible to the subject.
What kind of evidence will I receive?
A detailed report with timestamped photographs, video footage where possible, movement logs and a clear written account of observations.
How much does surveillance cost?
It depends on duration, number of agents and complexity. We provide a transparent quote after understanding your requirements during a free consultation.
