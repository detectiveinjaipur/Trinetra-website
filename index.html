Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is hiring a private detective in Jaipur legal?
Yes. Engaging a private investigator for legitimate purposes — matrimonial verification, background checks, fraud enquiry — is legal in India. We operate strictly within the law and never use illegal methods such as phone tapping.
Will my case stay completely confidential?
Absolutely. Confidentiality is the foundation of our work. Your identity and case details are protected by a signed non-disclosure agreement, encrypted records and strict need-to-know access within our team.
How long does an investigation take?
It depends on complexity. A background verification may take 3–5 days, while surveillance or matrimonial cases typically run 1–3 weeks. We share a realistic timeline before starting and keep you updated throughout.
What does an investigation cost?
Pricing is based on the type of case, duration and resources required. After a free consultation we provide a clear, written quotation with no hidden charges. You approve the scope before we begin.
Is the evidence usable in court?
Yes. We collect and document evidence — photographs, video, statements and records — in a manner designed to be admissible, and we provide legal support and testimony where required.
Which areas do you serve?
We are based in Jaipur and cover the entire city and Rajasthan, with the ability to coordinate investigations pan-India through our trusted partner network.
