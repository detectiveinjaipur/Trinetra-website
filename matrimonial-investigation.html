Matrimonial investigation in Jaipur.

Marriage is a lifelong decision. Our discreet pre- and post-marriage investigations help you make it with complete confidence.

Pre-Marriage

Verify before you commit.

Whether it's an arranged or love marriage, families in Jaipur trust us to quietly confirm that what's presented is the truth. We verify the prospective partner and their family with complete discretion.

  • Character & conduct — reputation, lifestyle, habits and social circle.
  • Financial standing — income, employment, debts and assets verification.
  • Family background — status, relationships and any concealed history.
  • Past relationships — existing or previous marriages and commitments.
Post-Marriage

When doubt needs an answer.

If something feels wrong, you deserve certainty — not anxiety. Our post-marriage investigations are handled with extreme sensitivity, protecting your privacy at every step.

  • Loyalty verification — confidential confirmation of fidelity concerns.
  • Activity monitoring — daily routine, contacts and movement patterns.
  • Evidence for divorce — court-admissible documentation when required.
  • Dowry & harassment cases — evidence gathering and legal support.

Matrimonial investigation FAQ

Will my spouse or their family find out?
No. Discretion is paramount. Our investigators work covertly and the subject is never made aware of the enquiry. Your involvement remains strictly confidential.
Can the evidence be used in a divorce case?
Yes. We document findings in a court-admissible format and can provide supporting testimony where required, in coordination with your lawyer.
How long does a matrimonial investigation take?
Most cases take between one and three weeks depending on scope. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your free consultation.
Is matrimonial investigation legal?
Verifying a partner's background and conduct through lawful means is legal in India. We never use illegal methods such as phone tapping or hacking.
